Process

How It Works

Three simple steps and attendance is done — for both students and lecturers.

1

Lecturer Starts Session

The lecturer logs in and generates a unique QR code for the class session.

2

Student Scans QR

Students open SmartAttend on their phone and scan the displayed QR code.

3

Location is Verified

The system checks the student's GPS location to confirm they're on-site.

4

Attendance Recorded

Attendance is instantly saved with timestamp, date, and location data.
